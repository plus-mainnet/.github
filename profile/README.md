## 🌐 About PLUS Mainnet
PLUS Mainnet is a high-performance Layer 1 blockchain designed for mass adoption, offering **sub-second finality** and **near-zero gas fees**. Our ecosystem bridges the gap between Web2 and Web3 by providing seamless interoperability with Ethereum and Binance Smart Chain (BSC) via the PLUS Bridge.

We are building a robust ecosystem tailored for DeFi, GameFi, and global DApps, starting with our flagship products: the **PLUS Hybrid DEX** and **K-Ohaeng**.

## 🚀 Key Ecosystem Products

### 1. [PLUS Hybrid DEX](https://plusmain.net/trade)
A revolutionary decentralized exchange that combines the speed of an off-chain order book with the security of an on-chain AMM.
- **Zero-Gas Swaps**: Execute trades without worrying about fluctuating network fees.
- **Auto-Bot Integration**: Algorithmic market-making and arbitrage bot support.
- **Real-time Settlement**: Powered by the PLUS Mainnet's high-speed block generation.

### 2. PLUS Bridge (Cross-Chain)
A highly secure, multi-sig vaulted bridge allowing users to instantly transfer USDT and WPLUS from Ethereum/BSC to the PLUS Mainnet natively.

### 3. Web3 Global Games & DApps
- **K-Ohaeng**: A unique cultural DApp integrating Saju (fortune-telling) and Web3 economy.
- **Global GameFi**: Upcoming tap-to-earn and high-speed crash games leveraging our zero-gas infrastructure.

At PLUS Mainnet, security and transparency are our top priorities. 
- **Multi-Sig Vaults**: Treasury and bridge assets are secured via institutional-grade multi-signature contracts.
- **Circuit Breakers**: Automated on-chain monitoring prevents anomalous fund movements.
